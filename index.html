Daegu Middle High School Hall of Fame

DMHS Hall of Fame Foundations

Founded in 2025 by Dylan Lentz, Savannah Torres, Alice Im, Tegan Pandell, and Heather Pak, under the principal Mr. Clits and vice principal Dr. Kelly.

To be inducted into the DMHS Hall of Fame, candidates must receive three recommendations from senior council members and one recommendation from the principal. This ensures that only those who truly exemplify excellence, leadership, and dedication to the DMHS community are honored.
